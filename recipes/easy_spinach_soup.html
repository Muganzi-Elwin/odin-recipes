Easy Spinach Soup

The above image shows the final finished dish of the Easy Spinach Soup Recipe

This yummy spinach soup is made with scallions and carrots in chicken broth. Top with Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • ½ cup chopped scallions
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • ½ cup orzo
  • 1 (10 ounce) bag fresh spinach, chopped
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat. Add carrots,scallions, and garlic; cook and stir until scallions are soft, 1 to 2 minutes. Pour in chicken broth; bring to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, 5 to 10 minutes. Stir in spinach; cook until tender, 2 to 3 minutes more. Season with salt and pepper.
